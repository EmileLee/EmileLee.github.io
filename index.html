Biography

Chenzhuo Li (李 陈卓) is a Ph.D. candidate of solid mechanics at the Beihang Photomechanics Lab (Prof. Bing Pan’s group). His research interests include digital image correlation, fluorescent imaging, composite materials, porous materials, and Microsystems.
